Our Story

Where Ancient Wisdom
Meets Modern Taste

Born from the rich heritage of Tamil Nadu, Karupatti Mittai is a celebration of traditional palm jaggery — Karupatti — reimagined as delightful chocolates for the modern palate.

VJS Food Industries was founded with a mission to bring the unmatched nutritional benefits of palm jaggery to every Indian household. Our chocolates are handcrafted in small batches, preserving the authentic flavour and mineral richness that makes Karupatti a true superfood.

We source palm jaggery directly from traditional farmers in Tirunelveli and Thoothukudi, ensuring the highest quality and supporting rural livelihoods. Every piece of Karupatti Mittai is a story of heritage, health, and happiness.

How It's Made

From Palm Tree to Your Hands

A time-honoured process that preserves every nutrient and flavour

STEP 01
🌴

Palm Sap Harvest

Fresh sap is collected daily from traditional palmyra trees by skilled local farmers.

→
STEP 02
🔥

Traditional Boiling

The sap is slowly boiled in iron vessels over wood fire to create pure palm jaggery.

→
STEP 03
🍫

Chocolate Crafting
